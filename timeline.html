My timeline so far

2017

My interest in computer science was first piqued after watching a YouTuber named Michael Reeves build various (useless) contraptions, like a robot that shines a laser in your eye, or one that detects when you're falling asleep and shoots energy drinks at you. Fascinate with what could be done with code, I then searched up "Easiest coding languages for beginners", where I found C++ as the first result. I accepted this result without any second thought, and began my learning.

2021-2024

During high school, I became an officer at my school's MESA club, which is a club that features several different STEM-based competitions, like building a wind-powered car out of basic materials, or a speed coding competition, which is what I specialized in.

2024

I only applied to 6 colleges, UC Merced, UC Irvine, UC Berkeley, UCLA, UCSD, and MIT for fun. This was a decision that came to stress me out greatly, as I thought that I only had a realistic shot at the first two and UCSD. After getting rejected from UC Irvine for Computer Science, my stress heightened, now unsure if I would get into UCSD. Luckily, that wasn't something I'd worry about for long, as later that month I received my acceptance letter and became a triton.
